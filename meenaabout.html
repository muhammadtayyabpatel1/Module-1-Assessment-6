About Us

Meena Fragrances - A Passion Well Pursued

Mr. Noorahmed was fond of fragrance and always wanted to live among aromas. His passion led him to create his own blend in 1964. With dedication and hard work, he successfully crafted his own fragrances. Starting with high-quality raw materials from India and later sourcing from international markets, he blended essential oils, natural extracts, and aroma chemicals to create soul-stirring fragrances.

A Deep Connection Between Sensation and Sentiments

Life is about spreading fragrance and making others’ lives fragrant. Late Mr. Noorahmed Gulamnabi Shaikh deeply believed in this philosophy. He made fragrances the essence of his life, building a thriving business now spreading its scent across the world.

The Legacy Continues

In 2005, Mr. Noorahmed passed away, leaving behind a thriving business. His four sons inherited his passion and legacy, expanding Meena Fragrances across India and beyond. In 2006, the brand was officially launched, offering luxurious roll-on fragrances. Today, Meena Fragrances offers over 60+ unique scents, sourcing high-quality raw materials from Europe and the US.
